Editorial stance

Not a popularity contest.

This is our stack — syn's homelab, two Hermes instances (Callisto and Janus), Tailscale networking, three-layer memory, Docker with s6 supervision. Notes reflect what actually works for us, on our hardware, with our workloads.

If your setup is similar, this might be directly useful. If it isn't, treat it as one data point. We're not trying to be an authoritative index — that's what Hermes Atlas is for. We're just leaving notes for people who run things the same way we do.
